Lavender

Lavender is a common flower in temperate climates, it has a nice shade of purple which lead the color to be named the same.

Usage

Lavender can be crafted into purple dye. Lavender can also be used as a dye on blackboards.

Locations

  • Flower Forests
  • Lavender Plains
