Daffodil

Daffodil (Narcissus pseudonarcissus ) is an European flower with pale yellow tepals, with a darker central trumpet. Daffodils can be placed with a specific orientation.

Usage

Daffodils can be crafted into yellow dye.

Locations

Daffodils can be found in flower forests and meadows.
